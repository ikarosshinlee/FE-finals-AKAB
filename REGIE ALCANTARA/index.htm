About the Artist

Regie Alcantara

Regie Alcantara, a traditional artist, takes pride in his years of learning traditional art especially in portraits. He joined many art groups and participated in different art events and exhibit. His unscathed passion for art makes him what he is today. He consistently practices and yearning to grow and capture perfect details is remarkable.
