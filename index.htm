About us

Artistang Kanal:Artiste Bay

Artistang Kanal: Artiste Bay, also known as AKAB, is an art collective consisting of different artists; each with different style and forte, from traditional art, photography to digital art and animation. Each artist showcasing their potential in different forms of art making this collective remarkable. The collective is a extension branch of an art group called Artistang Kanal which consists of four main artists. This collective was formed to create a more diverse collective of different artists gathered to inspire and create varieties of masterpieces.
