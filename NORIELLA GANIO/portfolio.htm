About the Artist

Noriella Ganio

Noriella Ganio illustrates out of hobby and rarely seen drawing to express her emotions. She excels in character design as she love creating stories and relationships between characters. She started out as an artist where she would create something out of imagination. As she hones her skills, she explores and trying out different art styles with the hope of creating a style that she could proudly call her own.
